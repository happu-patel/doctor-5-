Meet Dr. Ariganesh Chandrasegaran

Best Hair Transplant Doctor in Chennai

Dr. Ariganesh Chandrasegaran, a renowned dermatologist and award-winning hair transplant surgeon, has redefined excellence in hair restoration and skincare. A gold medalist in General Medicine, his expertise has been recognized internationally, with his work published in esteemed journals like Mycopathologia and the Journal of Cutaneous and Aesthetic Surgery (JCAS).

With over 9 years of dedicated practice, Dr. Ariganesh has successfully performed more than 3,000 hair transplants, catering to patients from India and abroad. He is a pioneer in advanced techniques like Bio Stimulated FUE Hair Transplant and Bio-Enhanced Simultaneous Transplant (B.E.S.T). Known for his proficiency in Giga sessions of FUE, he also specializes in beard and eyebrow transplants.

In addition to his hair restoration expertise, Dr. Ariganesh is widely sought after for dermato-surgical procedures and anti-aging treatments, including Botox and fillers. His commitment to patient care, combined with modern technology and precision, ensures unparalleled results and satisfaction.
